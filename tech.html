Clock Technology

The word 'clock' derives from the Latin word for bell, and the ringing of bells in central clock towers provided one of the first examples of time-based synchronicity for villagers. The clock has long been a social technology, a way of authorizing a singular source of information to propel collective activity. A clock is a machine in which a device that performs regular movements in equal intervals of time is linked to a counting mechanism that records the number of movements. All clocks, of whatever form, are made on this principle.A clock is a device used to measure, verify, keep, and indicate time.
